What do I do?

I am an independent consultant specializing in serverless technologies, cloud-native architectures, and developer advocacy. I would love to connect with you.

More about me

I am an AWS Serverless Hero and Head of Engineering at KonfHub, a one-stop platform for technical conferences built on AWS, primarily using serverless technologies.

My journey into serverless started with the AWS Chatbot Challenge, where I instantly fell in love with the power of Lambda functions and their limitless potential.

Beyond work, I am passionate about building and scaling serverless applications, sharing knowledge, and engaging with developer communities worldwide. I truly believe serverless is the future—making cloud computing accessible and empowering developers to build with minimal friction.

I actively contribute to the community through meetups, talks, and online webinars, and I founded the Tirupati AWS User Group and Bangalore’s first serverless-focused meetup, bringing together over 6,000+ members.

I also run Serverless Saturdays, a monthly webinar series dedicated to serverless technology, helping developers kickstart their journey and learn from global experts.
